FAQ

How does a TwitterChat work?

  • The moderators (Saron and Carlos) will post a question, like this one: "Q1: What are you favorite programming books? #CodeNewbie"
  • You respond to the question, like this: "A1: I loved Chris Pine's 'Learn to Program.' Great book!#CodeNewbie"
  • You can also respond to other people directly, like this: "@codergal what did you like about 'Learn to Program'? I've heard a lot about it! #CodeNewbie"
  • After some discussion and back and forth, roughly 5-10 minutes worth, Saron and Carlos will post a new question for the community to respond to.

How do I join the chat?

  • Follow the hashtag #CodeNewbie, and start tweeting!
  • Respond to one of the questions, labeled "Q1", "Q2", "Q3", etc, with a labeled answer "A1", "A2", "A3" etc.
  • Or respond to other people's tweets. Share a link. Tell a story. Talk about your coding experiences. Just make sure to include the hashtag, #CodeNewbie.

How can I follow the chat?

You can search for #CodeNewbie on Twitter and follow the stream, or you can go here for a more "chatroom" style experience.
